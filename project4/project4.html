Where Will You Go Next?

Every great trip starts with a little planning. Pick a destination, jot down what you want to see, and the rest tends to fall into place. This planner is a simple way to kick off the conversation with yourself before you start booking flights.

Five Tips Before You Go

  1. Check that your passport will be valid for at least six months past your return date
  2. Book flights on a Tuesday or Wednesday for better prices
  3. Save copies of your passport and ID to a secure cloud folder
  4. Tell your bank and credit card company your travel dates so they do not freeze your card
  5. Learn a few phrases in the local language before you go
